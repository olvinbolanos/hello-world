The following are posts that I have made to Promote Self-awareness for kids that are not eating right or are not aware of their diet patterns. Please feel feel to comment if you have any suggestions.

Making a difference one kid at a time

So many children have bad eating habits and do not know the conscequencers of their food diets. For example, children eat out almost 80 percent of the week and are malnutrition to the point of being overweight. These kids are becoming heavier and are not exercising enough throughout the day.

We have a moral obligation to help kids lose weight.

We cannot stand-bye and watch these kids lose interest in working out and enjoying fun active activities at the ball park or at the YMCA. We need to set the example as adults and find a sport that can instill a workout regimen into our childrens lives.

Trying to be the best role-moderl for the youth

Waking up early in the morning to start off the day with a great meal is as important as setting the mood. Our diet can either prolong our lifestyle or end it short. Making a great meal helps us maintain a healthy lifestyle and does not make us feel sluggish while we our at home, school, or at work.

Living a great life

We all have to make decisions on what we have to eat and those decisions either can make or break our diets. Cut down on Carbohydrates and junk food that does not give you the proper vitamins that your body so badly needs. We all need to live more healthier, why not start the challenge today?
